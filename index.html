2nd INTERNATIONAL e-CONFERENCE OF ULUL ALBAB

This conference aims to highlight the concept of Ulul Albab as a solution to the formation of a pious generation that is able to be the successor of leadership in the future. The conference will involve participants from within and outside of Malaysia such as Indonesia, Thailand, Brunei, Philippines and Singapore. Through this conference, UPSI will be able to highlight its role as the regional Ulul Albab reference centre.
